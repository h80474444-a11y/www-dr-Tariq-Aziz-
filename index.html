DR. TARIQ AZIZ

Associate Professor (Soil Science) | Regional Director (South Asia) INI

Professional Profile

Dr. Aziz serves as the Regional Director for South Asia at the International Nitrogen Initiative (INI) and holds the position of Associate Professor of Soil Science at the University of Agriculture, Faisalabad (UAF), Pakistan. He is the lead partner from Pakistan in the global project "South Asia Nitrogen Hub" and is also involved in the UNEP/GEF funded project “International Nitrogen Management Systems (INMS)”. [span_0](start_span)As a lead Editor, he has published “Nitrogen Assessment: Pakistan as a case study”.[span_0](end_span)


Dr. Aziz has published more than 120 research articles, and produced 48 M.Sc. and 5 Ph.D students in the discipline of Soil Science. [span_1](start_span)His research interests include improving fertilizer use efficiency, nutrient recycling, and nutrient pollution.[span_1](end_span)


Dr Aziz also served for about 7 years as Principal of UAF Sub-Campus Depalpur, Okara, and Project Director of two development projects worth Rs. 1300 million. [span_2](start_span)He has vast international experience and live collaborations in his field, and has organized and participated in various global Science Conferences.[span_2](end_span)
